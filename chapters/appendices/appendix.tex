\section{Reproducibility Notes}

All experimental work was conducted on a workstation with the following specifications: Intel Core~i7-12700K CPU (12 cores), 64~GB DDR4 RAM, NVIDIA RTX 3060 GPU (12~GB VRAM), Ubuntu 22.04 LTS. Random seeds were fixed at \texttt{42} for all stochastic components (data splitting, SMOTE neighbour sampling, model initialisation, dropout masks) to ensure reproducibility.

The end-to-end preprocessing and training pipeline executes in approximately 35~minutes on the above hardware, dominated by SMOTE oversampling on the training partition and XGBoost cross-validation. LightGBM training and SHAP explanation generation together account for less than 5~minutes of total pipeline runtime.
